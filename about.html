Our Philosophy

We're in this business because we love animals and their humans. We believe in going the extra mile to keep you and your pets happy and healthy. We aim for loyal lifetime customers, not just a one-time transactions. (Of course, if you’re in town temporarily and need a fur baby spa day, then we are glad to help!) We take care to use environmentally-friendly products for all of our services. We choose shampoos and other products that are not only safer for the environment, but safer for your pets health, and your health. We are committed to creating a safe, caring environment that brings out the best in you, your pets, and our team.

Our Team

We're highly selective and proud to feature some of the finest groomers in the Reno area. Our team brings collectively over 30 years of experience, with six groomers known for their attention to detail and expertise in handling of animals. No matter who you trust to care for your pet here, they're in good hands.

Owners: The Dias Family

Lisa and Bobby Dias took over the grooming salon on Kings Road in January 2020, realizing a long-held dream to own a grooming business.  They are longtime Reno residents and business partners.  Lisa has been in grooming for over 10 years, and worked in the very shop she now owns. She specializes in grooming techniques from specialty cuts to quality breed cuts, to mohawks and color. She takes care and her time with senior citizen and special needs doggies and puppies. There is no fur baby she can’t do and will try everything she can to pamper the aggressive kiddos, too! Despite launching their business in the midst of the COVID pandemic and potentially the worst circumstances ever, they are committed to build the best grooming shop in Reno to bring your dogs, and best place to work for top-quality groomers.  They live in Northwest Reno with their 10-year-old daughter, and their two dogs: Tiny and Pal Capone.  

About Our Business

We are a local family-owned business with fur babies of our own. With a combined experience of over 30 years among our groomers, we're exceptional in providing the grooming your pet requires and the love they deserve.
